Browser Permissions Audit

Complete disclosure of every Manifest V3 permission requested by Tabvora in the Chrome Web Store.

tabs Required

Why required: Necessary to read active tab titles, favicons, and URLs to display them within your local Tabvora dashboard.

What is NOT collected: Page contents, keystrokes, form entries, or cookies are never read or accessed.

tabGroups Required

Why required: Allows Tabvora to organize, collapse, color-code, and label tab groups natively inside Google Chrome.

What is NOT collected: No group configuration data is transmitted anywhere outside your device.

storage Required

Why required: Used to write your workspace names, custom colors, dashboard layouts, and user preferences to local browser disk storage.

What is NOT collected: Storage is isolated inside your browser instance and inaccessible to third parties.

sidePanel Optional / Ergonomic

Why required: Enables opening Tabvora seamlessly within Chrome's native side panel alongside your active web page.
