Voluntary Contribution • 100% Free & Open Source Project

100% Free & Open Source: Tabvora is and will always remain completely free with zero ads, subscriptions, or locked features. Donations are strictly voluntary contributions to support independent open-source maintenance.

Why Support Tabvora Development?

Building and maintaining a privacy-first browser extension requires continuous engineering effort, security reviews, and updates to keep up with Manifest V3 browser standards. Your voluntary support helps us:

  • • Keep Tabvora 100% ad-free and tracking-free.
  • • Continuously improve local workspace tools and dashboard performance.
  • • Maintain open-source codebases and process community feature requests.

Every contribution, no matter how small, directly empowers independent open-source development.
